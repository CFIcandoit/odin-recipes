Pancakes

Description

Delicious flour pancakes served with butter and maple syrup.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of flour
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 3 cups milk
  • 1 Tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 Tsp salt
  • 1/2 Tbsp butter
  • 3 Tbsp maple syrup

Steps

  1. Add 3 cups of flour to a large mixing bowl.
  2. Mix in 3 cups of milk
  3. Mix in 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  4. Mix in baking powder and salt.
  5. Whip until smooth.
  6. Heat griddle to 450 degrees.
  7. Pour batter 1/3 cup at a time onto griddle.
  8. Cook until pancake has bubbled completely on top. Then Flip.
  9. Cook other side for about one minute.
  10. Add butter and syrup and enjoy!
