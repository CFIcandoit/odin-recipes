Beef and Rice Casserole

Description

Ground meat (turkey or beef) on steamed rice covered with sweet and sour sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups of rice
  • 1 pound of ground turkey or beef
  • 1/3 cup of provolone cheese
  • 1/4 cup of sweet and sour sauce.

Steps

  1. Steam rice in covered saucepan until well cooked.
  2. Cook meat in oiled pan until at least 165 degrees.
  3. Serve rice on plate topped with meat.
  4. Add cheese and sause to taste and enjoy!
