Spaghetti

Description

Slightly overcooked rice spaghetti noodles covered with a spicy tomato sauce topped with mozarella and parmesian cheese with a side of grilled chicken

Ingredients

  • One box of rice-based spaghetti noodles
  • One 4.5oz can of tomato paste
  • 4.5oz of water
  • 1 Tbsp of italian seasoning
  • 1/4 cup of sugar
  • 1 Tsp crushed red pepper
  • 1/2 Tsp salt
  • 1/2 Tsp pepper
  • 1 8oz boneless chicken breast
  • 1/2 cup grated mozarella cheese
  • 2 Tbsp grated parmesian cheese

Steps

  1. Boil noodles in 2 gallons of water for 13 minutes.
  2. Put 4.5oz of tomato paste into small saucepan.
  3. Mix in 4.5oz of water until tomato paste is thin and smooth.
  4. Add seasoning, red pepper, salt, pepper and sugar.
  5. Warm saucepan over low burner.
  6. Cook chicken on grill or pan.
  7. Place cooked spaghetti on plate and add heated sauce.
  8. Add grated mozarella and parmesian cheese.
  9. Serve with cooked chicken.
